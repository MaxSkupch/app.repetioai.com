Frequently Asked Questions

What is a token?

A token is a unit of text used by AI systems to process and understand language. Think of it like a syllable in a word, but for AI. Tokens can be whole words, parts of words, or punctuation marks. In the English language, 100 tokens are roughly equivalent to 75 words. To learn more, you can read OpenAI’s detailed blog post on this topic here.

How are tokens counted?

Tokens are counted both in the prompts you send to the AI and in the responses generated by the AI. This means that your token usage includes the text you input as well as the text the AI outputs, both contributing to the token budget in your plan.

Before sending a request, you can see exactly how many tokens your prompt(s) will use and get a rough approximation of the number of tokens the response will require. After the request has been processed, you can see the exact total number of tokens used for that request.

Which plan do I need?

If you’re unsure which plan is right for you, we recommend starting with our Basic plan, which includes a 14-day free trial (no credit card required). This allows you to explore and understand your token usage without any commitment. If you run out of tokens, you can easily upgrade to a higher plan whenever it’s convenient for you.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes, we do. If our standard plans don’t meet your needs or if you have specific questions, please feel free to contact us at any time at contact@repetioai.com. We are open to discussing custom pricing options to better suit your requirements.
