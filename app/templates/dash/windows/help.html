How to change your time zone

Your timezone is updated on every login and synced to the timezone of your browser. If you need to update your time zone, just log out and then log in again.

Negative Token Balance

We allow users to maintain a negative token balance as a courtesy, ensuring text compositions can be completed without immediate interruption. However, if your balance becomes negative, you won't be able to start new compositions until you add funds and restore a positive balance.

To prevent abuse, if the negative balance exceeds a specific threshold, the composition may be stopped midway and delivered incomplete. To avoid this, we recommend keeping your token balance sufficiently funded for the expected length of your composition.

Unfortunately, predicting the exact token usage for a composition is not reliably possible, so we cannot warn you in advance if your balance is insufficient. As a rough estimate, expect one token to correspond to approximately 0.75 words (counting both the prompt and the response).
